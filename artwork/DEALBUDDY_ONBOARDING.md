# DealBuddy Onboarding - Algorithmic Philosophy

## Movement: "Warm Discovery"

### Philosophy

**Warm Discovery** is a generative aesthetic movement that captures the excitement of finding hidden treasures—specifically, the student deal-hunting experience. Each frame represents a moment of joyful discovery, where vibrant colors emerge from subtle beginnings and cluster together like friends sharing finds.

The algorithms embody the student experience: energetic particles represent the buzz of discovering a good deal, flowing lines chart the journey from browsing to claiming, and warm color palettes evoke the feeling of saving money with friends.

### Computational Principles

**Particle Emergence**: Deals appear like stars in a night sky—first subtle, then brilliant. The algorithm spawns particles that seek clustering, mimicking how students gather around shared discoveries.

**Flow Fields as Decision Paths**: Each user's journey through deals creates invisible paths. Flow fields visualize these decision journeys—the browse, the compare, the claim—represented as organic, flowing curves that guide the eye toward key actions.

**Seeded Personalization**: Every student's deal discovery is unique, just as each algorithm execution produces distinct but related compositions. Seeds ensure reproducibility while celebrating variation.

### Visual Language

- **Warm tones**: Amber (#F59E0B), coral (#F97316), soft pink (#FB7185) represent excitement and savings
- **Cool accents**: Teal (#14B8A6), indigo (#6366F1) for premium features
- **Gradient transitions**: From discovery to claim to share—the user journey mapped as color flow
- **Organic shapes**: No harsh edges—everything curves and connects, like the social nature of deal-sharing

### Craftsmanship Note

The algorithm is meticulously crafted to balance chaos and order—representing both the overwhelming choices of deal-hunting and the satisfying clarity of finding the right one. Each parameter has been refined through countless iterations, the product of deep computational expertise in creating warm, inviting generative experiences.
